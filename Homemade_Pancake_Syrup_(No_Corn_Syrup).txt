Name=Homemade Pancake Syrup (No Corn Syrup)

Ingredients:
    •    1 cup brown sugar (or white sugar for a lighter flavor)
    •    1/2 cup water
    •    1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    •    1 tbsp butter (optional, for richness)
    •    A pinch of salt

Instructions:
    1.    In a small saucepan, combine the brown sugar and water.
    2.    Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
    3.    Let it simmer for about 3-5 minutes until slightly thickened.
    4.    Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract, butter (if using), and a pinch of salt.
    5.    Let it cool slightly before serving. It will thicken as it cools.
