Name=Spicy Enoki Ramen Topping

A crunchy, spicy topping that adds texture to ramen.

Ingredients:
    •    100g enoki mushrooms
    •    1 tbsp oil
    •    1 tbsp Samyang Buldak sauce (or any spicy sauce)
    •    1 tsp honey (optional, for balance)
    •    ½ tsp sesame seeds

Instructions:
    1.    Trim the enoki and separate into strands.
    2.    Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the enoki until crispy (about 3-5 minutes).
    3.    Remove from heat and toss with Buldak sauce and honey (if using).
    4.    Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve over hot ramen.
